# ❤️ Romantic Relationships

Focused on nurturing love, communication, and partnership growth.

#### Subcategories:

* **Love Index & Compatibility Tracker** – Evaluate relationship health and compatibility patterns.
* **Relationship Status & Timeline** – Chronicle relationship milestones and changes.
* **Communication Logs & Love Languages** – Track key conversations and understand partner preferences.
* **Intimacy & Connection Prompts** – Offer suggestions to deepen emotional and physical connection.
* **Conflict Resolution Tools** – Frameworks and logs for managing and resolving disagreements.
* **Date Night Planner** – Plan romantic outings and shared experiences.
* **Shared Responsibilities (Finance, Tasks)** – Manage joint tasks and responsibilities.
* **Special Dates & Anniversaries Tracker** – Never miss key moments like anniversaries or birthdays.
