# ⚖️ Personality & Social Wellness

Promotes self-awareness, emotional balance, and sustainable social engagement.

#### Subcategories:

* **Introvert/Extrovert Insights** – Tools to explore social preferences and tendencies.
* **Social Energy Battery & Burnout Alerts** – Gauge emotional capacity for socializing.
* **Life Balance Tracker (Social vs Personal)** – Track time and energy spent across life areas.
* **Personalized Tips for Recharge** – Recommendations based on personality type and patterns.
* **Happiness & Social Well-being Index** – Combine metrics to understand social fulfillment.
* **Conflict Resolution Journal** – Safe space for reflecting on disputes and personal growth.
* **Gratitude & Reflection Journaling** – Daily gratitude and emotional reflection prompts.
* **Mental Health Resources (anxiety, social fatigue)** – Access to curated mental health tools and support.
